Tentang Saya

Hanni was born on October 6, 2004, in Melbourne, Australia. Her father is from Hanoi, Vietnam, and her mother is from Sai Gon. Her parents and grandparents emigrated to Australia some time before her birth, and Hanni and her family visited Vietnam during her childhood. Her younger sister Jasmine was also born in Melbourne in 2007.

Hanni was a fan of One Direction as a child, and was photographed holding a sign at one of their concerts in 2013. Her bias was Niall Horan. She also took part in Voice Kids Australia in the 4th grade. She was a Catholic as well.

In 2019, Hanni was part of an Australian K-pop dance cover group called AEMINA. In October 2019, she passed the Big Hit Global Audition and became a trainee in South Korea in 2020.
